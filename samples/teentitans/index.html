Teen Titans

Arcade-Style Minigames

A pair of minigames for the multi-platform console game Teen Titans which are homages to the early arcade classics Pong and Space Invaders. The player must complete each game in order to advance to the final boss battle.

Details

The purpose of this project was to add arcade-style minigames to the gameflow, both as an opportunity to briefly introduce a new game mechanic into the somewhat repetitive beat 'em up gameplay, and to accentuate the story element revealing that the Teen Titans are actually trapped inside a video game.

The minigames were initially scrapped by the development team due to time constraints, at which point I was asked by Majesco to visit the developer, integrate into the team and workflow, and become acquainted with their proprietary engine and toolset as quickly as possible in order to enable remote development for the remainder of the project.

Since lack of personnel was the reason cited for cancelling the minigames in the first place, development was to proceed with minimal time commitment from the existing team members. I was therefore called upon to implement almost all aspects of both minigames, including game mechanics, physics, AI, visual and audio effects, interface, etc. What resulted is two complete minigames produced single-handedly by myself, utilizing almost entirely pre-existing assets.
