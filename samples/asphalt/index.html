Asphalt: Urban GT 2 3D

Particle Effect Enhancement: Nitro Exhaust

A graphical improvement to the nitro exhaust particle effect to produce a more realistic and powerful visual effect.

Details

This is an example of a simple enhancement task which had a large impact on the final product. The player accumulates the capacity to use nitro "boosts" for brief surges of acceleration at up to three levels. The requirements for this task were simply to improve the nitro exhaust graphical effect, since the existing implementation was poor.

Asphalt: Urban GT 2 3D had no concept of a particle-generator or tool to accomplish such a task, and as such, all particle effects were hand-coded; the nitro effect being no exception. The original and improved versions can be seen below.

The sensation of speed and energy that the improved nitro effect produces should be self-evident. Consult the screenshots for guidance on what to look for.
